Written Screening — Q1 to Q5
Paste the candidate's written responses
Q1
Tell me about your last two jobs. What did you do, how long were you there, and why did you leave? — Reveals stability, self-awareness, honesty.
Q2
Walk me through a job where something went wrong on site. What happened and what did you do? — Tests problem-solving, accountability, field judgment.
Q3
What does a good day on the job look like for you? — Reveals motivation and work values.
Q4
Have you ever had to train or help someone newer than you on a job site? How did you handle it? — Signals leadership potential and patience.
Q5
What are you looking for in your next job that you haven't had in recent ones? — Reveals what they want and whether JCC can offer it.
